Each year, a select group of undergraduate students (we refer to this group as Focus Scholars) who are in their third through final years of undergrad from around the nation are invited to apply to attend Focus. All accepted scholars will be reimbursed for accommodations, meals, and transportation subsidiaries. During Focus, scholars will: Learn about Georgia Tech's graduate degree programs Learn about the financial resources that are available to them Visit academic units and research laboratories to connect with faculty, staff, and potential future mentors Get guidance on navigating the graduate school application process Network with more than 100 scholars with similar interests and goals Georgia Tech Degrees With graduates of Georgia Tech equipped for success in a world where technology touches every aspect of daily living, our bachelor's, master's, and doctoral degree programs are consistently recognized among the best. Degrees are offered through the Institute’s six colleges: Computing Design Engineering Ivan Allen College of Liberal Arts Scheller College of Business Sciences Offering graduate degree options in virtually every science and engineering-related discipline, as well as technology-focused degrees in areas such as business administration, public policy, international affairs, and economics, Georgia Tech provides excellent preparation for employment in the academic, government, and corporate arenas.
