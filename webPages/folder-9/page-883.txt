Peer-I-Scope aims to assist students in thriving within the academic and cultural environments of Georgia Tech while expanding their scope of influence and inclusion. Peer-I-Scope program is aimed at increasing the academic persistence and personal success of all participants. Peer-I-Scope participants will have various opportunities to Network with peers, corporate representatives, and GT Alumni Obtain social and emotional support from Ambassadors and OMED community Receive resources that assist with transitioning and matriculating through Georgia Tech Academic support and experiential learning assistance and so much more 2022-2023 Peer-I-Scope Ambassadors Join Peer-I-Scope Meet The Peer I Scope Ambassadors Peer-I-Scope Ambassadors support Achieve Atlanta scholars, 2nd-3rd year transfer students (particularly from HBCUs and community colleges), and graduate students.
