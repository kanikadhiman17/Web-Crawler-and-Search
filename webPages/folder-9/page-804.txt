Share event via these services Share GTMusic Revive - GT Symphony Orchestra Concert Thu Sep 22, 2022 7:30 PM - 9:30 PM (Time zone: Eastern Time US & Ca) Calendar Public Events Who GT Symphony Orchestra Where Ferst Center Show on map The GT Symphony Orchestra presents the world premiere of the original soundtrack for Archaelund, an orchestral suite by composer-pianist Laura Ballestrino. This is to be followed by Beethoven’s Piano Concerto No. 4, featuring Ballestrino on piano. The evening will conclude a performance of Sibelius’s Symphony No. 1, a work that draws upon lush, romantic motifs undercut by a persistent, chilly melancholy, marking the composer’s first foray into the world of symphonic music.
