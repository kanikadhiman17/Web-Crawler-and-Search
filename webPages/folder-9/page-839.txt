Advising Erin Jackson is the academic advisor for the School of Music. She helps students design a schedule that works for them, guiding them through the registration process and helping ensure that they stay on track. She is also available to troubleshoot general problems during the semester for students, such as ensuring proper access to classes or equipment, addressing academic concerns, or tending to general software needs.
