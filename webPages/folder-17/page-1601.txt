Participants will tap into their “innate brilliance” and generate personal insights. As desired, each insight is followed by a request for action to subsequently support a sustained focus and transformative shift in behavior and practice. International Coach Foundation (ICF) - Georgia Chapter "Holding Our Clients Through Holding Ourselves: Enhancing Intimacy and Inclusivity in Coaching" April 13, 2016 A coaches' ability to "hold space" for clients and be fully present with them can differentiate and deepen the connection with clients. In this interactive workshop, participants will learn about providing a space for coaches to courageously self-reflect, identify various dimensions of their individual diversity, and identify behaviors that enhance coaching impact.
