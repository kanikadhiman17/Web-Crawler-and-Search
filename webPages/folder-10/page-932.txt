We strive to provide excellent service to our applicants and recipients; administer funds and programs effectively and efficiently and in compliance with all regulations, rules, policies and procedures; exercise good stewardship of all funds entrusted to us; and serve as an advocate for the strategic financial support for students. Financial Aid Priority Deadline is January 31 Financial aid Applications for the 2023-24 academic year are available. All applicants, including HOPE & Zell Miller recipients, must reapply for aid each year to maintain eligibility for aid. Failure to apply may result in a loss of financial aid for the upcoming and subsequent years.
