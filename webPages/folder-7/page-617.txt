Our curriculum also incorporates opportunities to participate in interdisciplinary courses in addition to our rigorous discipline-specific courses. Curriculum Plans B.S. in Architecture Students engage with a range of cutting-edge and emerging topics of both theoretical concern and technical application. 2-Year M.Arch Those with a four-year preprofessional degree in architecture may apply for the 2-year M.Arch. 3.5-Year M.Arch Those with an undergraduate degree in a non-architecture discipline can apply to the 3.5-year M.Arch. MSUD This program is for those who wish to expand upon their previous professional education and/or experience. Architecture Certificates We offer four undergraduate architecture certification programs. Accreditation Georgia Institute of Technology, School of Architecture offers the following NAAB-accredited degree programs: Master of Architecture, 3-year track (non-professional degree + 102 semester hours) Master of Architecture, 2-year track (preprofessional degree + 60 semester hours) Next accreditation visit for both programs: 2022 Accreditation Information Student Work We are excited to share our virtual End of Year Show!
