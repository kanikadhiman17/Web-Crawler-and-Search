QS World Rankings DesignIntelligence Meet Our Ambassadors Our undergraduate and graduate ambassadors are faculty-nominated student representatives who embody the diversity, talent, and enthusiasm of our School. Our ambassadors are available to answer questions about our School and share their experiences about being a student at Georgia Tech. Read their profiles and send them an email. Our ambassadors will greet you when you visit and show you why Georgia Tech is the best place to study architecture. Pictured: Paola Santiago, Graduate Ambassador Meet Our Students Pre-College Program The Pre-College Design program consists of two-week online workshops in architecture and industrial design. Hosted by the College of Design at the Georgia Institute of Technology, this program provides the opportunity to engage with our world-class faculty and current students, and give high-school students insight on what it is like to study architecture and industrial design. High school students who have completed their freshman year or above are welcome to apply. Sign Me Up!
