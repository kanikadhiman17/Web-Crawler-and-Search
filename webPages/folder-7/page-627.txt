See Our Students' Work Studio Culture The National Architectural Accrediting Board (NAAB) introduced the requirement for a studio culture policy as an additional criterion for accreditation. The Studio Culture Policy looks at time management, intellectual diversity, theory and practice, collaborative design, constructive criticism, desk crits and pin-ups, and design reviews.
