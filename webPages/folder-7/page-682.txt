From north of Atlanta: Take Interstate 75/85 south to exit 249D, toward Georgia Tech/US-78/US-278/North Ave. Turn right onto North Ave. Travel less than .1 miles and turn right on Techwood Dr. Travel .3 miles and turn left on Fourth St. Travel .2 miles and the East and West Architecture Buildings will be on your right. Google Maps Directions Visitor Parking A parking deck, attached to the Georgia Tech Hotel and Conference Center, offers easy and convenient parking. It's accessible from Spring Street, and hourly rates are from $3 (for less than an hour) to $18 for the day. The parking garage address is 770 Spring St. NW, Atlanta, GA 30308-1031. Other Visitor Parking Areas Walking Directions from Parking Deck From the intersection of Spring Street and Fifth Street walk west over the Fifth Street Bridge toward main campus. Turn left at the second stoplight onto Fowler Street. Turn right at the stop sign onto Fourth Street. The East Architecture Building is at 245 Fourth Street, located on the right as Fourth Street comes to a dead end. Taking MARTA to Georgia Tech The Tech Trolley is available from the Midtown MARTA (Atlanta's rapid rail system) station to various points on campus. The North Avenue MARTA stop is located at the south end of campus. Exit the station onto North Ave and turn right to cross the connector and enter campus. MARTA Tech Trolley Routes Questions?
