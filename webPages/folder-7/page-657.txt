Meet Our Ambassadors Our ambassadors are faculty-nominated undergraduate and graduate student representatives who embody the diversity, talent, and enthusiasm of our School. Ambassadors are available to answer questions about our School and share their experiences about being a student at Georgia Tech. Read their profiles and reach out through email or follow them on Instagram. Our ambassadors will greet you when you visit and show you why Georgia Tech is the best place to study architecture.
