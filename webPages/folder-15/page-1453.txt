Howard Middle School's Girls Excelling in Math and Science (GEMS) club. This weekly after-school club welcomes girls interested in interactive science and engineering activities. David T. Howard Middle School is an Atlanta Public School located at 551 John Wesley Dobbs Ave. NE, Atlanta, GA 30312. The group meets every Tuesday from 4-5:30 p.m. (currently meeting virtually). Other sponsors for GEMS include the American Society for Mechanical Engineers and the Howard Middle School PTA. For more information about GEMS, contact Dr. Mary Lynn Realff.
