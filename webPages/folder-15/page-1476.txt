As part of Institute Diversity, the Center for Student Diversity and Inclusion encourages students to excel academically, interact across cultures and perspectives, and value inclusion. The purpose of the Center for Student Diversity and Inclusion is to: Serve students from all backgrounds and encourage them to be inventive leaders who think globally. Offer a positive, enriching environment to connect diverse students, faculty, staff, alumni, and community stakeholders through effective programs, projects, networks, and research. Diverse collaboration often sparks great innovation—creating the next solutions that will improve human life in a meaningful way.
