Share pictures, videos, or a description of your project through the form below! Please be sure to read the “MUST READ” document before uploading anything to this folder! Welcome! As of 2022, this will be the official process for FBD Programs to upload their documentation for projects, finished and/or in progress. We are delighted to have your FBD Program use this AIAS Google Drive folder as a space to share all the amazing things you have and will work on! As this is a collective, open method for all to use, there are a couple of points you must know on how to use and manage your documents here.
