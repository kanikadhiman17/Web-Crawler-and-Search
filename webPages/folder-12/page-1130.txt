Many thanks to all of our nominees, their colleagues and member institutions for all that they do to support our shared vision of advancing the culture and production of architectural research. Congratulations to all the award recipients! For a full description of the awards and recipients, please click here: Press Release_2022 ARCC Awards Program ARCC James Haecker Award for Distinguished Leadership in Architectural Research Henry Sanoff, North Carolina State University ARCC Book Award Jeffrey Hogrefe and Scott Ruff, Pratt Institute ARCC New Researcher Award Farhana Ferdous, Howard University ARCC Dissertation Award Tzu-Chieh Kurt Hong, Georgia Institute of Technology ARCC Research Incentive Award Caryn Brause, University of Massachusetts Amherst Christopher Meek, Teresa Moroseos, Heather Burpee, University of Washington Award recipients were selected by ARCC’s Board of Directors and Officers. Review and deliberation were consistent with rigorous peer-review where nominees and proposals were debated on their own merits. Past recipients of ARCC Awards can be reviewed on ARCC’s website (arcc-arch.org).
