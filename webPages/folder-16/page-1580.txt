Programs in this initiative include: Inclusive Leaders Academy – Offers a professional leadership and legacy development program for Institute managers driven by an innovative learning curriculum. One-on-one Coaching – Coaches executives and senior or emerging leaders on self-awareness and authenticity; self-mastery; self-care and resilience; and storytelling and legacy development based on Tech’s Transformational Learning Curriculum. Team Coaching – Coaches leadership teams on creating engaging and inclusive environments. Leading Women@Tech – Creates empowerment opportunities for women leaders and builds a community of women who will advance a culture of inclusive excellence.
