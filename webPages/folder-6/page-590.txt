Our School of Building Construction applies the design process to practical outcomes of the construction industry and life-long building management. One of the underlying principles of our School of City & Regional Planning is that efficient and human-friendly municipal environments need to be planned and designed. Our School of Industrial Design takes a humanist and forward-looking approach to designing things and environments, but they also use the design process to further integrate – even invent – technologies and objects.
