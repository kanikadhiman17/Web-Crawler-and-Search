Our design research drives innovation in education, practical approaches to living, and manifests as innovative products. From regional growth and planning to wearable technology, to evidence-based research and design to robotic musicians, we lead collaborative research by example at Georgia Tech and beyond. Our research is unique, and in many cases only happens here.
