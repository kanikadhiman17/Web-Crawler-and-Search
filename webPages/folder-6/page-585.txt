Our academic coursework and research emphasize the intersection of technology and design to inform and enhance people's lives. Often, this symbiotic approach reveals unique solutions to modern human needs. The College of Design offers three undergraduate degrees, nine master's degrees, and four doctoral degrees, along with minors and certificates.
