Staff Positions Georgia Tech offers careers for staff positions throughout the campus, located in administrative units and in the colleges and schools. Positions are available in the following areas: Finance, Administrative, Managerial, Engineering, Technical, Labor/Skilled, and Medical. Temporary positions are also available. Proceed to ServiceNow Research Positions Research positions are available in the Georgia Tech Research Institute (GTRI) and in various colleges and schools. GTRI hires, equips, and supports the best researchers in the most recognized fields of science and technology. To view current GTRI opportunities, visit the GTRI website or the GT Careers portal. View Research Opportunities at GT Careers portal Go to GTRI Jobs page Faculty Positions With a prestigious faculty holding one-hundred endowed chairs and professorships, as well as a staff of more than 4,500 to support all aspects of the campus, it's no wonder that Georgia Tech ranks number seven in the nation for best public universities according to U.S. News & World Report. Georgia Tech boasts 835 faculty members with 708 of those positions being full-time teaching faculty. Nearly 64 percent are tenured faculty, and 94 percent of Tech faculty hold doctoral degrees in their fields of expertise. Current faculty opportunities may be found by visiting the websites of each college or school.
