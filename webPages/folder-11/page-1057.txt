With an open and positive environment, the ensemble strives to develop its members as musicians and build a community that has you leaving each rehearsal wanting to come back for more! No audition is required, and weekly rehearsals run from 6:30-8:00 pm every Tuesday and Thursday in West Village 175. Treble Choir performs in at least one concert per semester and at other special events on the Georgia Tech campus, such as singing the national anthem for sports like basketball and volleyball.
