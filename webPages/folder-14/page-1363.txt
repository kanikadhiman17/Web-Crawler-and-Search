In addition, it serves as liaison to the Georgia Attorney General’s Office regarding litigation and other legal issues. The Vice Chancellor of Legal Affairs also serves as Secretary to the Board of Regents. The Office of Legal Affairs further includes the System Director for Equity and Investigations, who oversees System-wide compliance with Title IX of the Education Amendments of 1972. It also includes the University System Office’s Open Records Officer, who coordinates responses to requests for documents under the Georgia Open Records Act. The Office of Legal Affairs is located in the Trinity-Washington Building at 270 Washington Street in downtown Atlanta and can be reached by phone at (404) 962-3255 or by e-mail at usg-legal@usg.edu. This page includes information to assist with interactions with the Office of Legal Affairs as well as links to other helpful legal resources. Please understand that none of the information or links contained here constitute legal advice and that they do not establish an attorney-client relationship.
