Mental Health Initiative Strategic Plan 2024 UNIVERSITY SYSTEM OF GEORGIA ITS Customer Services Operations Division The single point of contact for technology support and service requests Home About Us Introduction Mission & Vision Contact Customer Services Submit Self-Service Support Request After Hours Support Information Support Communication Guidelines Service Level Guidelines Services & Support Information Emergency Web Service Welcome to the Information Technology Services (ITS) Customer Services and Helpdesk, the University System of Georgia’s (USG) single point of contact for support and service requests. ITS Customer Services and the Helpdesk provide a single point of contact, 24 hours a day, seven days a week to receive service requests and to log, track, and resolve support requests. A complete list of products and services currently supported through USG are available in our Service Level Guidelines. For emergency, business interruption, or other production down situations, immediately call the ITS Helpdesk (706-583-2001 or 888-875-3697 toll free within Georgia). Submit Support Requests Submit a new or view an existing support request. This could be an issue or a question about ITS current products and services, or it could be a request to propose new, enhanced or changed functionality for any of the service areas supported by ITS. Self-service support or service requests require a user ID and password; email helpdesk@usg.edu for more information and assistance.
