The University System of Georgia Foundation, Inc. is a philanthropic foundation that raises and manages donations to support our mission and goals. As the cooperative partner of the USG and a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, we are managed by a separate governing board of trustees. MISSION: To advance the work of the University System of Georgia, comprised of 26 public higher education institutions through leadership and engagement in philanthropy, asset management, and institutional advancement. VISION: To create a long-term, sustainable culture of philanthropy across the University System of Georgia for a multitude of initiatives and programs.
