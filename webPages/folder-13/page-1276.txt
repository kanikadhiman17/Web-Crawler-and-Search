This includes current and former USG employees. Assistance with OneUSG Connect - Benefits For questions related to coverage level changes (i.e. adding/removing spouse or dependents, dropping or picking up coverage), assistance with benefit plan design questions or pharmacy/medications (i.e. what is covered, claims payment, etc), changes in coverage (i.e. switching plans, enrolling in Medicare, etc) please contact OneUSG Connect Support at 1-855-214-2644 and follow the prompts to reach the OneUSG Connect – Benefits Call Center. You can also reach the OneUSG Connect – Benefits Call Center toll-free by calling 1-844-5-USGBEN (1-844-587-4236). Assistance with employment opportunities or questions about institution specific policies and procedures Please contact the institution's Human Resources department directly. A listing of USG institutions (including a link to the institution's website which contains a directory) is available at https://www.usg.edu/institutions/.
