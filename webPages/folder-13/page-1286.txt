OneUSG Connect is currently used by all University System of Georgia institutions. What can employees do in OneUSG Connect? Employees use OneUSG Connect to record and submit time, access, review and update personal information including direct deposit, pay checks and benefits anytime, anywhere – by computer, tablet, or a smartphone. How do employees access OneUSG Connect? All employees have access to the system securely with their established log-on credentials and password.
