This office provides advice, guidance and assistance to the members of the Board of Regents, the Chancellor, the Chancellor’s Cabinet, Presidents, Chief Business Officers and Chief Human Resources Officers of the University System of Georgia. This office also includes staff of the Associate Vice Chancellor for Total Rewards, who are responsible for the administration of all systemwide employee benefits programs. Additionally, this office includes the staff of the Assistant Vice Chancellor for Organizational Development. This team is responsible for developing and implementing systemwide leadership development initiatives. The Board of Regents of the University System of Georgia is a participating party in the Employment Eligibility Verification Program (E-Verify) effective December 5, 2012.
