PERM Requirements Please Note State law prohibits Georgia Tech from hiring private attorneys. Employees may not hire attorneys to file a PR petition naming Georgia Tech as the petitioner without express permission from Global HR. The Manager of Global HR or his/her designee is the only authorized signatory for a Georgia Tech sponsored employment-based permanent residency petition.
